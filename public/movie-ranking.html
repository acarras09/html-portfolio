The Best Movies According to Andres

Top 3 Movies in no Particular Order

Django: Unchained

From the great mind of Quentin Tarantino, this movie blends action, romance, and drama into a spectacle that will keep you on the edge of your seat the whole time.

The Dark Knight

From the cast to the sound direction, the whole movie comes together for a joyride of ever-building tension, eventually culminating in an ending going against the grain.

The Thing

Most people are not inclined to add horror movies to their, top movies lists, but I am not most people. "The Thing" is much more than just blood and gore (though there is plenty of that), it's a brilliant look into the minds of those faced with imminent death and deciding who to trust.
